Return & Cancellation Policy

1. Right of Withdrawal: Customers have the right to return purchased metal art products within 14 days of delivery without giving any reason.

2. Return Conditions: The product must be unused, undamaged, and returned in its original packaging. Return shipping costs belong to the Buyer unless the product is defective.

3. Refunds: Once the returned item is received and inspected, the refund will be processed back to the original payment method within 7-10 business days.
